The Tablets of Stone

A Two-Team Scripture Quest

✦ ✦ ✦

Long ago on Mount Sinai, the Lord gave Moses ten holy commandments. In every age since — from ancient Israel to the Nephites, from Joseph Smith to today — His people have shown what happens when we keep them, or break them.

✦ How to Play ✦

  1. Split into two teams: Team A and Team B.
  2. Each round, one team gets a story and a scripture to look up.
  3. Once they find the verse, they pick which commandment it teaches.
  4. Right answer? They earn a tablet for their team!
  5. Wrong answer? The other team gets a chance to steal — with a brand new scripture for the same commandment!
  6. Teams take turns going first. The team with the most tablets at the end wins!
